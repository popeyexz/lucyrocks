## 🎯 CONTENT PRINCIPLES (From Skills)

### 1. Humanizer Rules (APPLY TO EVERYTHING)
```
BEFORE: "Additionally, this product leverages cutting-edge features"
AFTER:   "Then it just works."

REMOVE:
- "additionally, furthermore, moreover"
- "delve, leverage, utilize" → "dig, use"
- "intricate, pivotal, crucial" → "works, changed, needed"
- "in order to" → "to"
- "due to the fact that" → "because"
```

### 2. Social-Content Templates

**Twitter:**
- Maximum 3 line breaks
- Hook in first 10 words
- 280 chars max
- Link in reply (not tweet)

**LinkedIn:**
- Personal story → business lesson
- No links in body (kills reach)
- Link in first comment
- 1,200-1,500 characters

**Instagram:**
- Photo: Product in use, no face
- Caption: 1-2 sentences
- Story: Behind-scenes

### 3. Marketing Psychology Triggers

| Trigger | Use In | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Zeigarnik | Twitter | "These 3 replaced 20..." |
| Social Proof | Email | "2,847 people" |
| Loss Aversion | Pricing | "Don't miss out" |
| Scarcity | Launch | "Only 3 this month" |
| Reciprocity | Free gifts | Template first, ask later |
