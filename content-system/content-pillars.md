## 🎣 HOOK FORMULAS

### 1. Contrarian (Breaks Pattern)
```
I stopped wanting to impress.

Everything changed.

The quieter the purchase, the louder it lands.
```

### 2. Specific Story (Social Proof)
```
3 years ago, I bought wrong.

Today: 14 products. 12 returned.

These 3 are still here.
```

### 3. Curiosity Gap (Zeigarnik)
```
This lives in my kitchen.

Nobody has asked about it.

But 4 people have copied it.
```

### 4. Loss Aversion
```
Most products steal time.

This one pays it back.

47 minutes a day.
```

## 🔮 PSYCHOLOGY TRIGGERS

### For Instagram/Twitter:
- **Mimetic Desire** — Show others wanting it
- **Social Proof** — "Copied 4 times, nobody asked"
- **Scarcity** — "Only 3 this month"

### For Email:
- **Reciprocity** — Give free template first
- **Bandwagon** — "2,847 people" not "a lot"
- **Commitment** — "Which do you prefer? A or B"

### For Pricing:
- **Anchoring** — Show ₹2,499, then "This is ₹899"
- **Charm Pricing** — ₹999 signals value
- **Rule of 100** — Under ₹100: "20% off". Over: "₹200 off"

## 📱 PLATFORM STRATEGY

### Twitter/X (3-5x daily)
**Best times:** 7am, 12pm, 5pm, 8pm IST

**Format:**
- Short sentences
- Maximum 3 line breaks
- Hook in first 10 words
- Link in reply (not tweet)

### LinkedIn (1x weekly)
**Best time:** Tuesday 8am IST

**Format:**
- Personal story → business lesson
- No links in body (kills reach)
- Link goes in first comment
- 1,200-1,500 characters

### Instagram (1x daily)
**Format:**
- Photo: Product in use, no face
- Caption: 1-2 sentences, no hashtags
- Story: Behind-scenes, polls
