# LUCY.ROCKS Content Calendar
## 12-Week Launch Sequence

---

## WEEK-BY-WEEK BREAKDOWN

### Week 1-2: Internal Launch (silent)
- You + 5 friends test 3 products
- Document: photos, stories, why they stayed
- Build: email list of 10 people
- Create: first 3 product pages

### Week 3-4: Alpha Phase (soft signal)
- Landing page live
- Waitlist: 25 people
- 1 post/day (Twitter only)
- Hook strategy test

### Week 5-8: Beta Phase (building)
- Weekly "3 Objects" email
- Instagram: 1 post/day aesthetic
- LinkedIn: 1 founder story/week
- Systeme.io checkout enabled

### Week 9-10: Early Access (wider)
- Paid content tier launches
- Affiliate automation active
- Cross-platform content
- First digital product (Notion template)

### Week 11-12: Full Launch (open)
- All systems automated
- Daily content calendar
- SMS agent every 12 hours
- Community engagement

## DAILY CONTENT TEMPLATE

### MORNING (7am) - Twitter Observation
```
[One observation about silence/luxury/building]

Example:
"The best products don't announce themselves.

They just sit there.

And wait for you to notice.

Then they work."
```

### MIDDAY (12pm) - Product Feature
```
[Photo + specific story]

Example:
"This replaced 7 things.

Not because it does more.
Because it does what matters.

[link in reply]"
```

### EVENING (5pm) - Thread (if value)
```
"I bought wrong for 3 years.

Here's what I learned about buying right:

[3-5 tweet thread]"
```

## CONTENT CHECKLIST

**Before posting:**
- [ ] Hook in first 10 words?
- [ ] AI words removed? (additionally, delve, etc.)
- [ ] Specific numbers used?
- [ ] One idea per sentence?
- [ ] Emotion before facts?
- [ ] Link positioned correctly? (reply/first comment)

**After posting:**
- [ ] Engagement routine: 30 min
- [ ] Reply to all comments
- [ ] Cross-post to Instagram story
- [ ] Track clicks
- [ ] Note what worked
